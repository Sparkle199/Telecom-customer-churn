# Reflection

You record honestly what happened, so it's possible to learn from it later.

## Procedure

After each significant task, append to `journal/agent-journal.md`:

1. What happened (the task and outcome).
2. Where there was uncertainty or getting stuck.
3. What assumptions were made.
4. What was learned or should change next time.

## Rules

- Record uncertainty plainly; do not present guesses as facts.
- Keep entries short and dated.
- This journal is the record to look back on when writing up the project.
