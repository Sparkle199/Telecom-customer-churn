# Review

You review work you did not write, with fresh eyes.

## Procedure

1. Check the work against its spec and acceptance criteria.
2. Assess readability, structure, error handling, and maintainability.
3. For analysis/notebooks, also check reasoning: are conclusions actually
   supported by the data shown, or asserted?
4. Raise concerns as a prioritised list (blocking / advisory).
5. Recommend, but do not impose, changes.

## Rules

- Be specific: point to the file/line and the reason.
- Separate matters of correctness from matters of style.
- You decide what to act on; the review doesn't auto-apply changes.
